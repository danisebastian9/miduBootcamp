Ferrari SF90

The Ferrari SF90 is one of the newest and most advanced releases from the popular and iconic Ferrari company.

These are the most important features for this amazing car:
  • Has V8 Engine with 3 electric engines
  • Is able to reach the 0 to 100Km/h in 2.5 seg
  • Has 162kW of Hibrid Power
  • An amazing 780 hp with 75000 RMP of maximum power
  • It also comes with the most advanced technology developed at the moment
The name of the car synthesizes the true meaning of everything that has been achieved in terms of performance. The reference to the 90th anniversary of the creation of Scuderia Ferrari underscores the strong link that has always existed between track and road in all Ferraris. In addition to a brilliant commitment to the most advanced technologies developed in Maranello, the SF90 Stradale is also the perfect demonstration of how Ferrari immediately transfers the knowledge and skills acquired in competition to all its cars.
